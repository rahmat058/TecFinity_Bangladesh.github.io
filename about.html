TechFinity Bangladesh

TechFinity Bangladesh is an IT networking & solution providing company and networking products supplier. TechFinity Bangladesh started its journey since 2012 as subsidiaries of PraDeshta Limited which is Pioneer Data Communication, Internet Service Provider (ISP) in Bangladesh since 1991. In 2015, TechFinity Bangladesh isolated from PraDeshta Limited with proprietorship license.


As the name itself is self-explanatory ‘Tech’ for Technology and ‘Finity’ for Infinite. We want reaching to the infinity through technology.


With the development of multi-media services and the integration of telecommunication, the new trends of business have made it mandatory for companies to deploy user-friendly and state-of-the-art of technologies.
